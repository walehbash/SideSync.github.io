Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties?
You can search for properties using our search bar on the homepage. Enter your desired location, property type, and price range. You can also browse all properties on our Properties page.
How do I schedule a property viewing?
Click on any property card and use the "Schedule Viewing" button. Our agents will contact you within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time for your viewing.
What documents do I need to rent a property?
Typically, you'll need proof of income, credit report, rental history, and government-issued ID. Our rental agents will provide a complete list based on your specific situation.
How do I list my property for sale?
Visit our Sell page and fill out the property submission form. Our team will review your information and contact you within 24 hours to discuss the next steps.
What are the current market trends?
Market trends vary by location. Our AI assistant can provide real-time market insights, or you can contact our agents for detailed market analysis for your specific area.
